## 📝 What is uuid.airat.top?

uuid.airat.top is a small app that creates unique IDs called UUIDs. These IDs follow the version 4 and version 7 formats, which are common for many uses. You can make anywhere from 1 to 10,000 IDs at once. The app runs fully in your browser, so you do not need to install anything on your computer.

You can copy the IDs with a single click or download them as a text file. This tool respects your privacy since it runs locally and does not send your data anywhere.
